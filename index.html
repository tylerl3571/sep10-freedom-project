Medical Practice

Everyone knows about medicine and how it helps everyone, but do you know how it helps?

The purpose of medicine is to help everyone that has a disease and try and treat people with certain illnesses. Medicine is also there to cure illnesses that are mysterious.

Medical Practice has been around for a long time, ever since people started healing others using herbs or medicine.

Back in the era of Ancient Egypt, Ancient Egyptians were the earliest recorded systems of medical practice. This has been used by 12 documents on a papyrus that includes the Ebers Papyrus
